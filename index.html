Privacy Policy for Valgrind Caller

Effective Date: April 2, 2026

Valgrind ("we," "our," or "us") provides the Valgrind Caller application as a professional communication tool. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with your use of our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

To provide high-quality VOIP and PSTN calling services, the App requires specific permissions:

  • Microphone: Required to capture your audio during calls. Audio may be processed via Amazon Chime and AWS Connect.
  • Camera: Used for video conferencing features and capturing profile images within the Valgrind ecosystem.
  • Contacts: We access your device contacts to allow you to initiate calls to existing saved numbers and sync them with your professional Valgrind CRM records.
  • Call Logs: We collect metadata (call duration, timestamps, and numbers) to provide history and analytics to your organization.

2. Authentication (WorkOS)

We use WorkOS for enterprise Single Sign-On (SSO). We do not store your corporate passwords. Authentication is handled via secure industry-standard tokens (OAuth/SAML) provided by your organization’s identity provider.

3. Third-Party Service Providers

We share information with third-party services only to the extent necessary to perform calling functions:

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS): Used for call routing (Connect), media sessions (Chime), and backend processing (Lambda).
  • WorkOS: Used for secure user authentication.

4. Data Storage and Recordings

If your organization has enabled Call Recording, audio files and AI-generated transcripts are stored securely within the Valgrind AWS infrastructure. These are used for professional record-keeping and CRM synchronization. Access to these recordings is governed by your organization's administrative policies.

5. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your communication. All media traffic (audio/video) is encrypted in transit using standard protocols (SRTP/TLS) via the Amazon Chime SDK.

6. User Rights

You may revoke permissions (Camera, Microphone, Contacts) at any time through your mobile device settings. However, doing so will significantly limit the app’s ability to function as a communication tool.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date."

8. Data Deletion & Account Requests

We provide users with the ability to request the deletion of their accounts and all associated data, including call recordings and AI transcripts. To submit a deletion request, please use our official form below:

Request Account Deletion

Upon request, your data will be permanently removed from our active databases and AWS infrastructure in accordance with our data retention policies.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact your Valgrind System Administrator or reach out to our support team.
